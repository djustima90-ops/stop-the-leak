Bynder's website is designed for enterprise SaaS lead generation and performs well in conversion elements like social proof, CTAs, and value proposition. However, it has significant lead leaks due to the complete absence of direct contact methods (no phone number, email, or live chat), forcing all interactions through a lengthy form.

Impact: The primary conversion action is buried as a plain text link, dramatically reducing click-through rate.
Fix: Add a prominent, styled CTA button (e.g., "Schedule Free Consultation") above the fold and repeat throughout the page.

Impact: Search engines and visitors lack a clear, immediate understanding of what the business offers, hurting SEO and clarity.

Impact: Visitors have no third-party validation of service quality, reducing trust and conversion rates significantly.
